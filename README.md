# Fourth & Go: Predicting NFL 4th Down Success
In my project titled "Fourth & Go," my group partner and I conducted a comprehensive analysis of NFL play-by-play data from the 2024 season to determine the most significant predictors of fourth-down conversion success. Utilizing R to source and clean data from nflfastr and SPSS to perform a hierarchical logistic regression, I modeled the probability of success based on two distinct blocks of variables: situational controls (such as yards-to-go, score differential, and Net DVOA) and active coaching decisions (such as play type, formation, and tempo).
